Trip Report Form

Welcome to the new trip report system. Please email me if you have any problems.

Please fill in the title, trip start date and duration fields below, then enter your trip report into the box labelled "Body of trip report". Add any photos, maps and GPX files (all optional) and finish by clicking the "Submit trip report" button at the very bottom of the page.

Entering your trip report directly into the box isn't a good idea, because if something goes wrong you might lose the lot. A much safer approach is first prepare your report on your own computer in your favourite editor, then copy and paste it into the panel below and then reformat as required. [If you use Microsoft Word to edit the original, you should use the icon at the top left of the edit panel - the one like a clipboard with a small 'W' in it - to paste your report.]

Photos (optional)

Please add at least a few photos if you have any; they give the report a big lift. Photos are laid out in rows of three, so it helps if you upload a multiple of 3 photos.

In this new Trip Report system you don't need to down-size images before attaching them; that will be done for you in the web browser. There may be a small delay after selecting each image while it is down-sized.

Route maps (optional)

Route maps are jpeg images showing the route you took, plotted on a map generated by a program like Freshmap or Topomap.
